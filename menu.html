Caffè Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a different flavor from traditionally brewed coffee.

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk foam. Variations of the drink involve the use of cream instead of milk.r

Caffè latte, often shortened to just latte in English, is a coffee drink of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk.

A caffè mocha, also called mocaccino, is a chocolate-flavoured warm beverage that is a variant of a caffè latte, commonly served in a glass rather than a mug.

If you like your drink creamy, smooth, and less intense, Café au Lait is the one for you! This French beverage is traditionally prepared with French-pressed and steamed milk. It contains equal parts of brewed Coffee and hot milk

The macchiato is an espresso coffee drink, topped with a small amount of foamed or steamed milk to allow the taste of the espresso to still shine through

Another popularly known drink is Frappuccino. Although its origin is an accidental one, today, Starbucks owns the trademarks for Frappuccino. It is an espresso-based drink consisting of a crème base blended with iced Coffee. However, other alternatives are also available that are caffeine free.

The macchiato is another espresso-based drink that has a small amount of foam on top. It's the happy medium between a cappuccino and a doppio
